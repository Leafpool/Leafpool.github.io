Extinct Arctic Animals

1. Woolly Mammoth

Woolly Mammoths are one of the animals that lived in the Arctic but now are extinct.

  • Very Shaggy and Furry So They Were Warm
  • Had Very Long Tusks That They Used For Digging, Collecting Food, Scaring off Enemies, and Looks

2. Woolly Rhinoceros

Woolly Rhinoceroses were also one of the animals that lived in the Arctic.

  • Lived Alone or in Very Small Groups
  • Had Horns to Scare off Enemies, like a Woolly Mammoth

3. The Great Auk

  • Looked slightly like a penguin
  • Became extinct because hunters hunted them

4. Irish Elk

Irish elk were one of the animals that was alive during the Ice Age.

  • Only ate plants
  • Had Very Large Antlers
